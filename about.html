The Location-Based Charity Finder

Hey, my name is Stacey and I've spent too much time reading the news recently.

So much so that I've gotten very motivate to help others. I've donated to charities in the hopes of making a difference but in the end I found that I was more connected to the rest of humanity than before. After spending some time thinking I decided that the best thing for me to do is volunteer when I can, where I can for the amount of time that I can. This will help me make a difference not only by supporting a worthy charity but by allowing me to see that I can make a difference and inspire others to do the same.
