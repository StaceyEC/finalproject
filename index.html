Where Do You Want to Volunteer Today?

searchFind Charities

I believe everyone should have the chance to make a difference. That's why I wanted to make it easy for regular people and nearby charities to quickly and easily connect. Volunteer Me is a pet project of mine after I realized that I kept putting aside my desire to give back in order for me to manage an aggressively busy life. I see the need for people to do good, I want to do good but the level of effort to give was too high, especially for young professionals who dedicate most of their waking lives to improving their careers.

As stupid as it sounds, donating to charities or volunteering for organizations shouldn't be hard. The work the organizations may ask can be hard, but finding nearby reputable causes that we want to give energy to shouldn't be, so I wanted to create a product that allows someone with an extra free day or afternoon to find an easy way to give back.
